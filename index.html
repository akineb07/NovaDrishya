About Us

Crafting moments
that endure

APEX Productions is a full-service production company headquartered in New York with a west-coast studio in Los Angeles. We work across theatre, film, television, and live events — bringing together some of the most talented creative minds in the industry.

Founded in 2008 by artistic director Elena Marchetti and executive producer David Osei, we have grown from a two-person team into a 40-strong company with global reach. Our work has been recognized at the Tony Awards, Sundance, BAFTA, and Edinburgh Festival.

We believe great stories have the power to challenge, heal, and transform. Every production we undertake is a commitment to that belief.

Our Vision

We believe in the radical power of live storytelling — that a single shared evening in the dark can shift the way we see the world, each other, and ourselves.
🎭

Artistic Integrity

We never compromise on creative vision. Every production begins with a strong artistic point-of-view and a commitment to bringing it fully to life, without shortcuts.

🌍

Inclusive Storytelling

We actively seek out stories from underrepresented voices and build teams that reflect the full diversity of the world we live in. Our stage is for everyone.

⚡

Fearless Innovation

From immersive staging to cutting-edge technical design, we push boundaries. We treat every production as an opportunity to advance what live performance can be.
