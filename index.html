Who We Are

Born from passion,
built on craft.

NovaDrishya — meaning "new vision" — is an independent production team pushing the boundaries of indie cinema and live theatre.

We believe that the most powerful stories come from intimate spaces. Our small, dedicated crew allows us to move fast, take risks, and stay fiercely true to our artistic vision — without compromise.

From emotionally charged theatrical performances to bold indie films, we create work that lingers long after the curtain falls and the credits roll.

What We Do

Two worlds,
one voice.

🎬

Indie Films

Character-driven narratives shot with an intimate lens. We develop, produce and distribute short and feature-length films that challenge genre conventions and celebrate human complexity.

🎭

Theatre Plays

Live performance that breathes. From original scripts to bold reinterpretations of classics, our productions turn every stage — big or small — into a charged, unforgettable space.

✍️

Original Scripts

Story is everything. Our in-house writers develop original works across both mediums, with a commitment to authentic voices, regional stories, and perspectives rarely seen in mainstream media.

How We Work

The anatomy of a production.

01

Story First

Every project begins with a story worth telling. We develop scripts through deep research, lived experience, and iterative collaboration.

02

Assemble

A lean, committed crew is curated for each project — directors, actors, cinematographers, designers — united by a shared artistic purpose.

03

Create

We shoot or rehearse with intention and flexibility. Small crew means every person matters, every decision is deliberate.

04

Release

Films go to festivals, streaming, and private screenings. Plays tour intimate venues where the audience and performer can truly connect.
